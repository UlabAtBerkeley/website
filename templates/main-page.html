  • Welcome to Research!

    We believe that every student interested in participating in research at Berkeley should have the opportunity to do so. We are committed to individually helping new students find research on campus, developing students led research projects, and connecting & supporting students already involved in research.


    Spring 2018 applications are live!

Research Labs Run by Undergraduates

Our lab members engage in collaborative research projects focused on improving and exploring the Berkeley community through the scientific method. By completing a “micro” semester or year-long research project students will learn the research process through guided workshops in a supportive environment.

Immersing New Students In Research

Members also attend workshops, go on tours of campus research labs, and talk with professors about their projects. All ULAB students will learn specific technical research skills: looking for academic papers, scientific computing, and brief scientific presentations.

"It can be one of the most transformative and fruitful adventures of their college career."

When Berkeley undergraduates engage in immersive research experiences, it can be one of the most transformative and fruitful adventures of their college career. Yet, many new students are deterred from even getting started. This problem stems from a tendency for programs to favor already experienced students and difficulty for new students in navigating a complex myriad of Berkeley resources. ULAB emphasizes new ways to reach underrepresented and socioeconomically disadvantaged students by partnering with the residence halls and existing organizations. -- Big Ideas @ Berkeley

Our organization is entirely student-run. Want to join?

From experienced student Principal Investigators to new students working as researchers in one of our labs, we offer a variety of positions for undergraduates interested in expanding their skill sets.
